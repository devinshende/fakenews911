About Us

Oct. 27th, 2022

We have created this website to debunk the conspiracies spread through fake news surrounding the 9/11 attack. Specifically we focus on the conspiracies and fake news surrounding antisemitism, the world trade centers architecture, if the government made 9/11 happen on purpose, if the government allowed for 9/11 to happen, and the anthrax attacks after the attack.

Created By: Annabel Truong, Devin Shende, Isabelle Granados Johnson, Jocelyn Lopez, Luci Anderson, Nikita Kamat, and Shahra Ellawendy
